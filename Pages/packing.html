Packing Essentials

Having the right equipment and gear can be the difference between a great camping trip and a complete disaster. Here is a list of essential items needed to survive the elements:

  • Map and Compass or a GPS
  • Winter Coat, Jacket, Hat, and Gloves
  • Water Bottle or Camelbak
  • Tent with Foam Ground Pad
  • Flashlight
  • Matches, Lighter, or a Fire Starter
  • First-aid Kit
  • Pots, Pans, and Cooking Utensils
  • Camping Stove

Keep in mind that your packing list might change depending on what types of activities you plan on doing during your camping trip.
